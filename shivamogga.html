WELCOME TO SHIVAMOGGA DISTRICT

Shivamogga district, also known as Shimoga, is a part of the Malnad region in Karnataka, India, known for its lush greenery and rich natural beauty. It's often referred to as the "Gateway of Malnad" and is famous for its abundant rainfall, forests, and waterfalls.
