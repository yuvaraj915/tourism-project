WELCOME TO CHITRADURGA DISTRICT

Chitradurga district is an administrative region in the Indian state of Karnataka, known for its historical forts, ancient sites, and picturesque landscape with unique rock formations. Located in the northern part of the state, it is named after the "Chitrakaldurga" hill and includes the city of Chitradurga as its headquarters.
