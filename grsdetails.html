GRS Fantasy Park

GRS Fantasy Park is a popular amusement park in Mysuru city, offering a wide range of thrill seeking rides, family rides, games and entertainment options.

  • Thrill rides: Aqua racer, Aqua tornado, Pendulum Slide, Crazy Cruise, Amazonia, Colombia, Music Bob and Dragon's Den are the intense, adrenaline pumping rides at the Park
  • Family Rides: Snow Sledge,Swing Chair, Dashing Car, Float Slides, Red Indian falls, Wave Pool, Aqua Dance Floor and Hawaiian Paradise are the relatively slow paced, fun filled rides for all family members
  • Kids Rides: Aqua trail, babytrains, play area, kids pool etc
  • Facilities: Changing rooms,restaurants, locker rooms, gift shop, conference rooms etc
  • Timings to visit the Park: GRS Fantasy Park is open from 10.30 AM till 6 PM on Monday to Saturday and 10.30 AM till 7.30 PM on Sundays and national holidays. It is recommended to spend a full day at Fantasy park.

How to reach the Park:

The Park is on the outskirts of Mysuru city (7 kms away). Mysuru is well connected by air, road and rail from Bengaluru (150 kms away). From Mysuru city GRS Park may be reached using a taxi.

Stay: Mysuru city has hotels and resorts in all budget categories.
